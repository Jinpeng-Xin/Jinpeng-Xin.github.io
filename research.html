Building Smarter Underground Systems

My research focuses on the intersection of Civil Engineering and Artificial Intelligence. I aim to bridge the gap between physical tunneling machinery and digital intelligence through Deep Learning and Digital Twins.

Focus Areas

AI for Geotechnics

利用机器学习与深度学习处理 TBM 掘进过程中的海量数据，实现参数预测与地层感知。

  • TPI Prediction
  • Time-Series Analysis

Digital Twins

基于 WebGL/Three.js 构建地下工程的实时数字孪生系统，实现施工现场的“人-机-环”可视化交互。

  • WebGL / Three.js
  • Real-time Monitoring

Risk Management

结合工程地质力学与数据驱动方法，对隧道施工中的塌方、岩爆等风险进行智能识别与预警。

  • Risk Identification
  • Safety Engineering
